Introduction

As a senior and very enthusiast computer engeneer I have worked extensively on some tools dedicated to high availability and performance.

Presentations and courses that I offer are not certified qualifications. My intention is primarily to share advanced experiments that I have had with these technologies.

Cost

Presentations and introductions are free, no matter they are done in a associative or professional context. I request only support for transportation costs and accommodation. Presentations are an opportunity to learn more about a technology and to understand when it applies (and when it doesn't)

If after a presentation you are interested in going further then the Training aim to detail how each of the tools fit into the life cycle of an IT project: design, development, operation, testing, etc ...
